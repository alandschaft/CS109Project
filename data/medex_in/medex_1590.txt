To be enrolled patient must have averaged at least 4 partial seizures per month with no 21-day seizure-free period during the 2 months preceding randomization To be randomized the patient must have had at least 3 seizures during the prospective Baseline Phase 28 days with no 21-day seizure-free period This should be documented in the form of medical history medical records or photocopied records of the patient diary/patient chart Simple partial seizures without motor signs will not be counted towards this inclusion criterion
