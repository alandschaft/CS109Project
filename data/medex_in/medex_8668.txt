Tissue will be collected from the operation theatre only after having obtained the consent and in strictest accordance with histopathological requirements i.e after assessment of resection margins It might be necessary to retrieve the tissue from the pathologist after first pathological assessment is completed Tissue will be placed in solution gassed with 95 O2 and 5 CO2 for functional and RT-PCR or Western blot experiments or in OCT optimal cutting temperature compound snap-frozen in liquid nitrogen and stored in a freezer until cutting for immunohistochemical experiments
