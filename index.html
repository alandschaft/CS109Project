Background

Standard medical treatment does not always work perfectly well for each patient. These patients wish to be informed about innovative new treatments that are tested in clinical trials. Moreover, doctors may whish to inform their patients about clinical trials that are relevant to their health problem and advise them about the risks and opportunities related to these trials.

Descriptions of clinical trials are publicly available, for example in clinicaltrials.gov. However it is a hard task to query those descriptions and quickly find clinical trials that are relevant to a patient with a certain condition, as they contain unstructured data. Particularly inclusion and exclusion criteria are written in natural language as it is important for the publishers of clinical trials (research hospitals, pharmaceuticals) to describe them in their own words.

Our mission is helping patients and their doctors on one side and the research hospitals and pharmaceuticals on the other side to find clinical trials match.
